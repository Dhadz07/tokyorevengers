SEASON 1

Hanagaki Takemichi (Yuki Shin) is unemployed, and is shocked to learn that his ex-girlfriend from junior high school Tachibana Hinata (Azumi Waki), has been killed in a conflict between the Tokyo Manji Gang, and is pushed in front of a train in the ensuing conflict.

SEASON 2

Tokyo Revengers Season 2 will be exclusively broadcast on Disney+, with international airing one hour after Japan's telecast.

SEASON 3

Tokyo Revengers season three will adapt the manga's penultimate story, the Tenjiku arc. Without spoiling too much, the Tenjiku Arc will see Takemichi return once again to the present only to find — you guessed it — that things still haven't gone as smoothly as he'd have liked
